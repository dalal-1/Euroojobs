Tips for Writing Great Job Postings

Do:

  • Be specific about responsibilities and expectations
  • List required skills and experience clearly
  • Include salary range to attract more applicants
  • Highlight your company culture and benefits
  • Use clear, concise language without jargon

Don't:

  • Use vague or generic descriptions
  • List too many "required" skills that aren't truly necessary
  • Use discriminatory language or requirements
  • Make the job sound more glamorous than it is
  • Forget to include basic information about the role
